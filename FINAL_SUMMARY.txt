A: "I built a self-correcting reasoning system using GRPO and RLVR that 
   improved logic accuracy by 26.4% while maintaining general knowledge.
   [See: PORTFOLIO_GUIDE.md for full talking points]"

Q: "What's GRPO?"
A: "Group Relative Policy Optimization normalizes advantages within groups
   instead of globally, reducing variance in policy gradients. This enables
   more stable learning with smaller batch sizes."

Q: "How do you prevent training collapse?"
A: "Two mechanisms: (1) KL penalty keeps policy close to reference model,
   (2) RLVR penalty tracks velocity and adds cost when metrics change too
   quickly, indicating instability."

Q: "How would you scale this to production?"
A: "I've already built it for production: YAML configs, structured logging,
   Docker deployment, CI/CD pipelines, and monitoring integrations."
